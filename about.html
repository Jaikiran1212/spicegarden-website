Our story

Cooking from the heart
since 2018.

SpiceGarden started as a small family kitchen in RS Puram. Today it's Coimbatore's favourite spot for authentic Chettinad food — but nothing else has changed.

How it began

A grandmother's
recipe book.

It started with a handwritten notebook — 140 recipes collected over 60 years by Paatti Meenakshi, our founder's grandmother. She grew up in Karaikudi, in the heart of Chettinad, where every household had its own spice blend and every meal took the better part of a day to prepare.

"Good food cannot be rushed. The spices need time. The fire needs patience. The guests deserve both."
— Paatti Meenakshi

When Arjun Murugesan opened SpiceGarden in 2018, he brought that notebook, those spice blends, and two cooks who had worked in Chettinad homes for decades. Six years later, the notebook still sits on the kitchen shelf. We still grind our own spices each morning. Nothing has changed — except the number of people who now know the secret.

What we believe

Food is more than
ingredients.

🌿

No shortcuts

We grind our own spice blends every morning. We never use pre-mixed powders. Shortcuts show up in the taste.

🍔

Local sourcing

Ingredients come from within 200km — Nilgiri vegetables, Coimbatore poultry, Ramnad chillies. We know our farmers by name.

♥️

Family first

Every dish is cooked the way it would be for family. Generous portions, honest flavours, and no dish that we wouldn't eat ourselves.

♨️

Slow food

The best Chettinad dishes take four to six hours. We plan accordingly — so you never eat something that was rushed.
